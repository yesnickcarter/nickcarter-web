# Agentic Novel — Process Summary

In one evening, we went from "I want to finish this draft before camping on Monday" to a fully operational drafting system that produced three chapters.

The session covered:

- **Built the drafting skill** — a reusable specification (SKILL.md) that loads context in three layers, enforces 12 prose rules, tracks plotline continuity, and generates chapters to a consistent quality bar
- **Removed a core premise** — decided Graham shouldn't start disgraced, rewrote 24 planning files to reflect him starting at the height of his power (stronger fall, stronger story)
- **Added the two-stage breaking point** — Graham doesn't become murderous all at once. Minty's death + being framed for her murder = the system he trusted weaponized against him
- **Planted a seed in chapter 3** — the assassination-as-negotiation line, a throwaway that pays off when Graham starts killing
- **Redesigned the father-daughter relationship** — Minty's tension with Graham is philosophical (anti-capitalism vs. pragmatic wealth), not the overused absent-father cliche
- **Resolved the POV system** — first-person rules, third-person grammar. Sloane gets interior access. Graham gets interior access when it's his chapter. Nobody else. Ever.
- **Defined the narrative voice** — McCarthy's brevity, Vonnegut's campfire warmth, Sloane's personality. Not invisible prose (Brown/Crichton). Voice on every page.
- **Created voice guides from scratch** — Minty (privileged, verbose, Tony Hawk's daughter energy) and Mara (chameleon with personas, T2 terminator quality)
- **Drafted chapters 1, 2, and 3** — using the skill, with full context loading, ban list enforcement, and post-draft reporting

Total time: approximately 4 hours. Total files changed: 29. Three chapters drafted. One novel-length system ready for the remaining 32 chapters.
